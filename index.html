Spreading Law

A spreading law refers to the relationship between the contact angle of a drop and the speed of its contact line. This project analyses real experimental data from the spreading of picolitre ink droplets on a flat substrate. From the results of the analysis we can determine the appropriate spreading law that best describes this interaction.
